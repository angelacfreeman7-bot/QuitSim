## 4. Description (4000 chars max)

```
Ever sit at your desk and wonder... could I actually quit?

Not someday. Not "when I win the lottery." Could you quit THIS year — and be financially okay?

QuitSim answers that question in 60 seconds. No sign-up. No spreadsheets. Just drag three sliders and watch the math unfold.

HERE'S HOW IT WORKS

Drag your salary, savings, and monthly spending into three simple sliders. QuitSim instantly runs 500 Monte Carlo simulations — the same math hedge funds use — to stress-test your finances against market crashes, surprise bills, and plain bad luck.

You get:

- Your Quit Readiness Score (0-100%) — a single number that tells you how prepared you are
- Your Freedom Date — the earliest month you could realistically quit
- A Stress Test — how your plan holds up when life throws curveballs
- Your Financial Runway — exactly how many months your money lasts

EXPLORE "WHAT IF" SCENARIOS

Curious what happens if you go freelance? Move somewhere cheaper? Pick up a side gig? The What-If Explorer lets you drag sliders and instantly see how each change moves your Freedom Date. Save your favorite plans and compare them side by side.

Built-in presets make it easy:
- Job Loss — what if you got laid off tomorrow?
- Freelance Ramp — what if you built up clients first?
- Early Retirement — the full FIRE scenario
- Baby + Travel — life changes that shift the math

DAILY CHALLENGES TO KEEP YOU MOVING

QuitSim isn't just a calculator — it's a daily practice. Each day you get a bite-sized challenge: review a subscription, research a side hustle, learn about index funds. Complete them to earn XP, build streaks, and watch your score climb over time.

Categories include saving, earning, learning, and mindset — because quitting your job is as much about confidence as cash.

FUN MODE (UNLOCKABLE)

Hit a milestone and you unlock Dream Mode — fantasy scenarios like "What if I won the lottery?" or "What if I moved to Bali?" Complete with confetti. Because planning your escape should be fun.

YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS

QuitSim runs 100% on your device. No servers. No accounts. No tracking. Your financial numbers never leave your phone. Delete the app and the data is gone — that's it.

We built QuitSim because we believe everyone deserves to know their number. Not the number a financial advisor wants to sell you a product around — YOUR number. The one that tells you how close you really are to living on your own terms.

This is an educational tool, not financial advice. But it might be the most honest financial conversation you've had in a while.

Your freedom date is waiting. Find it.
```
