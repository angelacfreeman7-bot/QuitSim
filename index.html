Features

Everything you need to plan your exit

Your Freedom Date

Not a vague "someday" — an actual calendar date when you can walk away, calculated from your real numbers.

Monte Carlo Stress Test

The same technique hedge funds use. Hundreds of randomized scenarios so you see the best, worst, and most likely outcome.

Runway Calculator

Month-by-month projections of your savings, investments, and expenses. Know exactly when the money runs out — or doesn’t.

What-If Scenarios

Freelance pivot? New baby? Market crash? FIRE? Adjust the sliders and see what happens to your timeline instantly.
