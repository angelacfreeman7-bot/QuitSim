Frequently Asked Questions

Is my financial data safe?

Yes. All your financial data is stored locally on your device and never sent to our servers. Simulations run entirely on-device. If you delete the app, your data is permanently removed.

What is the Quit Confidence Score?

It's a 0–100 score that combines three factors: your runway (how many months your money lasts), Monte Carlo simulation results (stress-tested outcomes), and your savings ratio (savings vs. annual expenses). A higher score means greater financial readiness.

How accurate are the simulations?

QuitSim uses Monte Carlo methods to run hundreds of randomized scenarios accounting for market volatility, expense shocks, and income variation. While no simulation can predict the future perfectly, this approach gives you a realistic range of outcomes rather than a single guess.

What does QuitSim Pro include?

Pro ($59/year) unlocks 500 Monte Carlo simulations (vs. 100), unlimited saved plans (vs. 3), couples mode for dual-income households, AI narrative insights, and PDF export.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → QuitSim Pro → Cancel Subscription. Your Pro features remain active until the end of your billing period.

Can I use QuitSim with my partner?

Yes! Couples mode (available in QuitSim Pro) lets you combine two incomes, savings, and expense profiles into a single simulation for more accurate household planning.

What does the Freedom Date mean?

Your Freedom Date is the projected date when your financial position is strong enough to leave your job with confidence, based on your current savings rate, expenses, and investment growth.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email us at support@quitsim.it.com with a description of the issue and your device model/iOS version. Screenshots are always helpful!
